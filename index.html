About Me

Hello, my name's Lara. My story is pretty curious. When I had to choose a highschool path I first wanted to study in the IT field, but in the end I opted for the artistic one. So I graduated with a lot of knowledge about video editing and photography but it wasn't what I wanted to do in my life so I was really stressed out while thinking about my future. This is why I started looking around for new experiences and then one day I discovered the magic world of Ux Design and Web Development. First I attended an Ux Design course but it didn’t fire me up at all because I didn’t love the graphic designer part. Then, after a few weeks, I found out about the front-end role and fell in love. So I started learning the basics through online courses while my interest was growing more and more. And that’s it, this was how I started my journey in this beautiful world of codes. It’s pretty hilarious how I ended up in the informatic field anyway.

After a lot of practice I understood that nothing doesn't work without a reason so I’m working a lot on my debugging skills and I’ve resolved almost all bugs in my projects on my own until now. Searching on the internet or doing a recap of every step to find the error.
